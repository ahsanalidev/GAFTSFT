1. ETHICAL DECLARATION (STATEMENT OF ETHICS)
====================================================================
This study did not involve human participants, animal subjects, or the
collection of any personal, private, or identifiable data. All experiments were
conducted on the publicly available TOFU benchmark, which consists entirely of
synthetic, fictitious author profiles created specifically for unlearning
research. No information about real individuals was used or processed. The
authors confirm that the work was carried out in accordance with accepted
standards of research integrity and good scientific practice.

====================================================================
2. AI USE DECLARATION
====================================================================
Generative AI tools were used primarily to assist with drafting and language
editing of the manuscript. All research ideas, the experimental design, the
implementation and code, the experiments, the resulting data, and the analysis
and interpretation of results are the authors' own original work, carried out
by S. A. Ali under the supervision and direction of M. U. Farooq. The authors
reviewed, verified, and edited all AI-assisted text and take full responsibility
for the entire content of the manuscript. Generative AI was not used to generate,
alter, or fabricate any data, results, figures, or citations, and no AI tool is
listed as an author.

3. CONFLICT OF INTEREST STATEMENT
====================================================================
The authors declare that they have no known competing financial interests or

4. DATA AVAILABILITY STATEMENT
====================================================================
This work uses the publicly available TOFU benchmark. The source code,
experimental configuration, and the result files that support the findings of
this study, including the per-seed evaluation metrics, the membership-inference
outputs, and the behavioral-audit data, are openly available in the project
repository at https://github.com/ahsanalidev/GAFTSFT. No restricted or
proprietary data were generated during this study.
